About Shuborno


Shuborno Biswas is a Bengali-Canadian guitarist and composer, based in Waterloo, Canada.

He is a member of the Kitchener-Waterloo Guitar Orchestra, and has composed several pieces debuted in virtual concerts by the orchestra.

He also enjoys performing as an amateur soloist, accompanying his family of violists, or as a duo with his vocalist and violist daughter Lilia.

Shuborno's compositions are inspired by rock music and his love of electric guitar. Decades before picking up a classical guitar, Shuborno played rock music on steel string and electric guitars since the age of 16.

Shuborno keeps himself busy as a Sr. Principal Software Engineer and a Karate Black Belt.
